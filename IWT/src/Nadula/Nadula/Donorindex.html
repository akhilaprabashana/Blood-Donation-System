"Your little effort can give others
a chance to live life"

Blood donation is all about gifting life to other people. It is a great act of kindness.
One pint of blood is sufficient to save 3 lives. One cannot make blood,
its a gift we all have inside of us to give to those who are in need. Every healthy person should donate blood.
People less than 18 and over 60 cannot donate blood. A donor must be at least 47.5kg by weight.
The donation process is a pain-free process that runs for about 10-15 minutes.
So don't be afraid, help us to save an innocent life.
